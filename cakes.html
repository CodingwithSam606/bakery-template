About Bakery

Tusam Bakery has been spreading the joy of fresh, quality baking since 2011. What began with supplying top supermarkets like Addide has grown into a beloved neighborhood bakery known for its dependable service and delicious treats. We specialize in baking for birthdays, weddings, and all kinds of events—always fresh, always reliable.
