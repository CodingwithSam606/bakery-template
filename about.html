Our Story

In a quiet corner of the city back in 2011, Tusam Bakery opened its doors with a simple dream: to bring warmth and joy to people through freshly baked bread and pastries. What started as a small kitchen baking loaves for local supermarkets like Addide quickly turned into something more.

Over time, customers weren’t just looking for bread—they were looking for moments. So, we began crafting custom cakes for birthdays, baking love into every wedding order, and adding sweetness to events of all kinds.

Today, Tusam Bakery is no longer just about supplying shelves—we’re about making memories. We take pride in being reliable, creative, and committed to quality. From our ovens to your table, every bite tells our story.

About Bakery

Tusam Bakery has been spreading the joy of fresh, quality baking since 2011. What began with supplying top supermarkets like Addide has grown into a beloved neighborhood bakery known for its dependable service and delicious treats. We specialize in baking for birthdays, weddings, and all kinds of events—always fresh, always reliable.
